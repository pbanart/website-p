Project Description



In my view, the therapeutic quality of making art acts as a panacea for the mental health that I have been struggling with for the last couple of years. After losing many of my loved ones I have been dealing with grief and loss while being unable to go to my home country and be with my family. This multi-media series is centered around my experiences of dealing with grief, memories, and nostalgia under social isolation.
